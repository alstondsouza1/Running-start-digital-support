# Future FAQ Content Notes

Survey feedback identified additional topics students want in the portal. These
items are documentation placeholders only. Staff should verify the wording,
contact details, links, and process steps before publishing them as FAQs.

## Suggested FAQ Backlog

| Priority | Audience | Suggested question | Information to confirm |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| High | Current | How do I contact my assigned Running Start advisor? | Official appointment link, drop-in schedule, office location, and what information students should include. |
| High | Current and Future | What should I do when enrollment or registration is not working? | Common holds, error messages, ctcLink troubleshooting steps, escalation contact, and screenshots that do not expose student data. |
| High | Future | What documents are required before I can enroll? | Enrollment Verification Form, placement records, signatures, deadlines, accepted formats, and secure upload location. |
| Medium | Current and Future | How do I find Running Start offices and student services on campus? | Building names, room numbers, accessible routes, parking or transit links, and an official campus map. |

## Content Review Checklist

Before an administrator publishes one of these FAQs:

- Confirm the answer with the Running Start Department content owner.
- Use an official Green River College URL where possible.
- Add a reviewed date and assign a person responsible for future updates.
- Avoid individual staff schedules unless there is a maintained source.
- Explain acronyms and use plain language.
- Include a phone or email fallback when a web process fails.
- Test every link on desktop and mobile.
- Do not include private student information or screenshots containing student IDs.

## Suggested Ownership Fields

Track these details outside the student-facing answer:

- Content owner
- Reviewer
- Official source URL
- Last reviewed date
- Next review date
- Publication status
